TEAM INTRO

At XBrain, we value the kind of open, direct communication that takes us to good places. We’d like, therefore, to extend you the same courtesy by telling you straight about what we hope for in a new team member:

We’d like it if you were open to new ideas.

We’d like it if you were a self-starter.

We’d like it if you had enthusiasm to spare.

We’d like it if you had good work ethic and discipline.

We’d like it if you considered conversation integral to building relationships between colleagues.

We’d like it if you erred on the side of overzealousness.

We’d like it if you respected the varied ideas of others as much as you’d like your own ideas to be respected.

We’d like it if you weren’t averse to stepping outside your comfort zone, whether that be something related to your area of work, or a completely different craft, or a salad topping you’ve never tried before.

We’d like it if you could sustain a symbiotic relationship with your work.

We’d like it if you could think about what we could contribute to your personal goals, as well as what you could contribute to our culture.

POSITION


Software Engineer

Daria is our answer to the ever-growing demand for accessible machine learning software across various industries. Every member plays a significant role in making this answer as functional and optimal as possible.

As a software engineer at XBrain, you would be taking charge of constructing Daria’s interface, which enables our users to combine / modify data in real time, and takes care of dataset and trained models management. Daria’s API server, which allows the trained models to be used in RESTful API, is also in your hands.

We look for the following qualifications in our candidates:

Industry experience (or experience of equivalence) with Web Framework (Python Django, Ruby on Rails, Java and Scala Play, Java Spring)
A bachelor’s degree in computer engineering / science, or experience of equivalence


We’d welcome the following qualifications:

Knowledge of and experience with software and systems related to Version Control, Issue Tracking, Continuous Integration such as Git, JIRA, and Jenkins. (*required for experienced hires)
Experience with Cloud Architecture (e.g. Amazon AWS, MS Azure, Google GCP), (*required for experienced hires)
Varied experience with JavaScript Library
Knowledge of Continuous Build, Integration, Test, and Delivery System

OFFICE LIFE

Although the yet-miniscule size of the team mandates that we each lead our own agendas, we try our best to take interest in the projects of others.

Attention to every detail of Estu’s construction is integral to a community as small as ours. Each member tries to stay cognizant of the role of the other in the team, so that we all have a comprehensive and specific understanding of everyday operations.
